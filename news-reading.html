Core Course Modules

A comprehensive step-by-step curriculum to make you broadcast-ready.

1. Teleprompter Mastery

Learn to read smoothly while maintaining eye contact with the camera. Overcome hesitation and build unbreakable flow for non-stop live feeds.

2. Voice Modulation & Tone

Understand how to inflect your voice for breaking news vs. standard broadcasts. Control pitch, breathing, and pacing perfectly under pressure.

3. Live Reporting Simulation

Roleplay dropping into live feeds. Learn to speak unscripted, take cues intelligently from the gallery, and handle live technical errors gracefully.

4. Studio Interviewing

Techniques for co-anchoring, moderating multi-person debates, reading body language, and conducting hard-hitting 1-on-1 studio interviews.
