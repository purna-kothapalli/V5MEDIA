Core Camera Modules

A hands-on, hardware-intensive curriculum with real industry cameras.

1. DSLR & Cinema Camera

Understand lens mechanics, aperture, ISO, and frame rates. Master focus pulling and exposure control on Blackmagic, Sony, and Panasonic broadcast setups.

2. Three-Point Lighting Setup

Learn to set Key, Fill, and Backlights for studio interviews. Study gels, reflectors, and outdoor lighting challenges for field production.

3. Multi-Cam Production

Experience how national news channels operate. Coordination with the PCR (Production Control Room), talking to directors via IFB, and switching.

Learn tracking shots, master the DJI Ronin systems, and understand movement-based storytelling for documentaries and high-end digital content.

Overview of our Camera & Lighting Training in Hyderabad

Our Camera & Lighting course in Hyderabad is a 90-day, technical immersion into the world of professional cinematography and broadcast production. You will master the mechanics of high-end digital cameras, from sensor physics to lens selection, ensuring you can capture stunning visuals in any environment.

The program places a heavy emphasis on the "Art of Lighting." You will work in a fully equipped studio, learning to manipulate light and shadow to create depth, mood, and professional-grade texture for both news broadcasts and cinematic productions.

Ideal for aspiring cinematographers and studio technicians, this course provides the technical foundation and industry-standard certification required to work on professional film sets and television studios.
