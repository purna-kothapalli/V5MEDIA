Core Journalism Modules

A hard-hitting curriculum designed for the modern-day reporter.

1. Mobile Journalism (MOJO)

Master the power of storytelling using your smartphone. Learn professional recording, mobile editing, and live news streaming from any location.

2. Investigative Techniques

Learn how to dig deeper. Study research methods, public records access, source protection, and the ethics of uncover reporting.

3. Live Field Reporting

Get comfortable in front of the lens. Practice P2C (Piece to Camera), managing live crowds, and reporting under various weather and lighting conditions.

4. Script Writing & Flow

Structure compelling news stories. Learn the inverted pyramid style, bullet-writing for tickers, and the differences between broadcast and print scripts.
