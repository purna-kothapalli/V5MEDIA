Core Editing Modules

A software-heavy curriculum focusing on logic, flow, and visual impact.

1. Adobe Premiere Pro Mastery

Master multi-cam editing, complex trimming, proxies for high-res footage, and the dynamic link to After Effects for seamless workflow.

2. Visual Effects (After Effects)

Learn motion tracking, keying (green screen removal), particle effects, and compositing to create professional television promos and openers.

3. DaVinci Resolve Color Grading

Understand the \'look\' of the film. Study color theory, LUTs, secondary color corrections, and giving a cinematic feel to standard footage.

4. FCPX for Professional Work

Dive into Final Cut Pro X, the industry favorite for professional editors. Learn Magnetic Timeline and roles for highly efficient media management.
